\section{4.3-1}
Let us assume $T(n)\leq cn^2$ for some c. we get: \newline
$T(n)\leq c(n-1)^2+n=cn^2-2cn+c+n$ \newline
and if we pick c=1, we get: \newline
$n^2-2n+1+n=n^2-n+1\leq n^2$ for $n\geq 1$ \newline
Hence, $T(n)=T(n-1)+n$ is $O(n^2).$
